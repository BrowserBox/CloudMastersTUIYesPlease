Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before getting started.

How does the free trial work?

Download BlueDot and use all features free for 24 hours. No credit card required. After 24 hours, choose a plan or the app gently reminds you to upgrade. Your data and configs are never deleted.

What happens after my update period ends?

Your license works for 3 years! During this time, you'll receive new features and cloud provider updates. After 3 years, you can purchase a renewal to continue using BlueDot with the latest updates.

Do you store my cloud credentials?

Never. BlueDot wraps your existing CLI tools (az, gcloud, aws, hcloud, vultr-cli). Your credentials stay in those tools, managed by you. We never see them.

What if a cloud provider changes their API?

Since we wrap official CLIs, you just update your CLI and BlueDot keeps working. We also push updates for major changes during your update period.

Can I use this for client work?

Absolutely! Many consultants and agencies use BlueDot to manage multiple client accounts. The Team license includes features specifically for this use case.

What's your refund policy?

All sales are final. We offer a fully functional 24-hour trial so you can verify BlueDot works for you before purchasing.
